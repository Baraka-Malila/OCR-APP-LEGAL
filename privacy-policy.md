# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [06/01/2025]

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our OCR App ("the App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Image Data
- The App may collect and process images that you explicitly select or capture.
- Images are used **only** to extract text through OCR and are **not stored or shared** unless stated.

### b. Usage Data
- We may collect anonymous usage data such as crash logs and performance metrics to improve the App.

### c. API Usage
- If you opt to use the Google Cloud Vision OCR engine, your image may be sent securely to Google’s servers.
- Google’s use of data is subject to [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 2. How We Use Information
- To extract and return recognized text from images.
- To improve the accuracy and performance of the App.
- To comply with legal obligations if necessary.

## 3. Data Retention
- We do **not** store your images or extracted text.
- Temporary files used during processing are automatically deleted after use.

## 4. Third-Party Services
- **Google Cloud Vision API** (Optional): Images sent via HTTPS for text recognition.
- **Tesseract OCR** (Default): All processing is local on your device.

## 5. Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your information.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 7. Your Rights
You can request deletion of app data or revoke access to any third-party services from your device.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
